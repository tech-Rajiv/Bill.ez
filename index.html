About

- THIS PROJECT

Story? behind this project

During Diwali, I ran a 10-day firecracker shop, and it came with a host of challenges. This project became my first and most crucial solution, pushing me to solve a real-life problem with technology.

what was the challanges?

With 50+ product varieties, managing operations wasn’t seamless:

- Staff struggled to memorize prices.

- Customers doubted verbal pricing and often bargained aggressively.

- Competitors nearby (within 200 meters) made it harder to stand out.

- Handling cash payments created risks of mismanagement.

I needed a solution that was efficient, transparent, and easy for my team to use.

What was the idea?

Keeping the challanges in mind i wanted to developed a Sales Management Web App with a focus on:

- Core Functionality – Streamlining products, catagories management, totals calcs, orders, querys, history, database records, payment workflow etc

- Mobile-Friendly UI – Designed specifically for mobile usage, ensuring a seamless experience for staff. Features include:
